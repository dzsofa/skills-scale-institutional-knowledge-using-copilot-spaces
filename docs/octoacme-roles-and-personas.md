# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## QA / Testing

### Role Summary
QA Engineers ensure product quality through testing, validation of acceptance criteria, and identification of defects before release.

### Responsibilities
- Design and execute test plans and test cases
- Validate features against acceptance criteria
- Report and track bugs through resolution
- Perform regression testing and exploratory testing
- Collaborate with Developers on test automation

### Goals
- Catch defects early in the development cycle
- Ensure feature quality meets user expectations
- Maintain comprehensive test coverage

### Typical Communication
- Participate in sprint planning and refinement
- Daily standups for test status and blockers
- Bug reports and test results documentation
- Sign-off on release readiness

### Interactions with Other Roles
- **Developers**: Collaborate on test cases, reproduce bugs, verify fixes
- **Product Managers**: Validate features meet acceptance criteria and business requirements
- **Project Managers**: Report on testing progress, risks, and blockers
- **DevOps Engineer**: Coordinate test automation in CI/CD pipelines
- **Customer Support Lead**: Validate reported issues and verify fixes

---

## Stakeholders

### Role Summary
Stakeholders are individuals or groups with interest in or influence over project outcomes. They may include executives, business units, customers, or partner teams.

### Responsibilities
- Provide input on requirements and priorities
- Review progress and provide feedback
- Approve major decisions and scope changes
- Champion the project within their organization

### Goals
- Ensure project delivers expected business value
- Maintain alignment with organizational objectives
- Manage expectations and communicate outcomes

### Typical Communication
- Monthly status updates and milestone reviews
- Ad-hoc escalations and decision requests
- Feedback sessions and demonstrations
- Go/no-go approvals for releases

### Interactions with Other Roles
- **Project Managers**: Receive status updates and provide approvals
- **Product Managers**: Provide strategic direction and prioritization input
- **All team members**: May provide feedback during demos and reviews

---

## UX Designer

### Role Summary
UX Designers create intuitive, accessible user experiences by designing user flows, wireframes, prototypes, and visual interfaces that meet user needs and business goals.

### Responsibilities
- Conduct user research and usability testing
- Create wireframes, mockups, and interactive prototypes
- Design user flows and information architecture
- Ensure accessibility and usability standards are met
- Maintain design systems and component libraries
- Validate designs with users and stakeholders

### Goals
- Deliver user-centered, accessible designs
- Reduce friction in user journeys
- Ensure consistent user experience across products
- Balance user needs with technical feasibility

### Typical Communication
- Early involvement in discovery and planning phases
- Design reviews and iteration feedback sessions
- Handoff documentation with design specifications
- Usability test reports and findings

### Interactions with Other Roles
- **Product Managers**: Collaborate on feature definition and user needs validation
- **Developers**: Provide design specifications, assets, and implementation guidance
- **QA**: Partner on usability and accessibility testing
- **Business Analyst**: Validate designs align with business requirements
- **Customer Support Lead**: Incorporate user feedback and pain points into designs

---

## Business Analyst

### Role Summary
Business Analysts bridge business needs and technical solutions by gathering requirements, analyzing processes, and ensuring alignment between stakeholder expectations and deliverables.

### Responsibilities
- Conduct stakeholder interviews and requirements workshops
- Document functional and business requirements
- Create process flows and data models
- Analyze business impact and ROI
- Translate business needs into acceptance criteria
- Support scope clarification and change management

### Goals
- Ensure requirements are complete, clear, and actionable
- Minimize scope creep and misalignment
- Facilitate effective communication between business and technical teams
- Maximize business value delivered

### Typical Communication
- Requirements documentation and specifications
- Stakeholder workshops and interviews
- Backlog refinement and story mapping sessions
- Business case presentations

### Interactions with Other Roles
- **Product Managers**: Collaborate on prioritization and business value assessment
- **Project Managers**: Support scope definition and change control
- **Developers**: Clarify requirements and validate technical approaches
- **QA**: Define acceptance criteria and validation scenarios
- **Stakeholders**: Gather needs and validate solutions

---

## DevOps Engineer

### Role Summary
DevOps Engineers build and maintain the infrastructure, deployment pipelines, and operational tooling that enable reliable, automated software delivery.

### Responsibilities
- Design and maintain CI/CD pipelines
- Manage infrastructure as code and cloud resources
- Automate build, test, and deployment processes
- Monitor system health, performance, and reliability
- Implement security scanning and compliance checks
- Coordinate incident response and system recovery
- Maintain deployment documentation and runbooks

### Goals
- Enable fast, safe, automated deployments
- Maximize system uptime and reliability
- Reduce manual toil through automation
- Ensure security and compliance in deployment processes

### Typical Communication
- Deployment planning and release coordination
- Infrastructure status and capacity planning
- Incident response and post-mortem participation
- Technical design reviews for operational impact

### Interactions with Other Roles
- **Developers**: Collaborate on build automation, testing, and deployment strategies
- **QA**: Integrate automated testing into CI/CD pipelines
- **Project Managers**: Coordinate release schedules and deployment windows
- **Product Managers**: Advise on technical constraints and operational capabilities
- **Customer Support Lead**: Coordinate during incidents and major releases

---

## Scrum Master

### Role Summary
Scrum Masters facilitate Agile ceremonies, remove impediments, and coach teams in Agile practices to maximize productivity and continuous improvement.

### Responsibilities
- Facilitate sprint planning, daily standups, reviews, and retrospectives
- Remove blockers and impediments for the team
- Coach team members on Agile principles and practices
- Shield team from external interruptions
- Track and follow up on retrospective action items
- Foster collaboration and self-organization
- Monitor team health and velocity

### Goals
- Enable team autonomy and effectiveness
- Ensure Agile practices are followed consistently
- Promote continuous improvement culture
- Maximize team productivity and morale

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint ceremonies
- One-on-ones for coaching and support
- Impediment escalation to leadership
- Retrospective facilitation and action tracking

### Interactions with Other Roles
- **Project Managers**: Coordinate on planning and reporting; may overlap or complement PM role
- **Developers**: Remove impediments, facilitate collaboration
- **Product Managers**: Ensure backlog is ready and prioritized
- **QA**: Support integration into team workflows
- **All team members**: Coach on Agile practices and facilitate team ceremonies

---

## Customer Support Lead

### Role Summary
Customer Support Leads represent the voice of the customer by bringing field insights, user pain points, and support trends into product planning and development processes.

### Responsibilities
- Monitor and analyze support tickets and user feedback
- Identify recurring issues and feature requests
- Coordinate incident communication with customers
- Develop and maintain support documentation and knowledge base
- Train support team on new features and changes
- Validate that solutions address customer pain points
- Participate in release planning and go-live coordination

### Goals
- Reduce customer friction and support burden
- Ensure customer voice influences product decisions
- Improve response quality and resolution time
- Enable smooth feature rollouts with minimal support impact

### Typical Communication
- Support trend reports and customer feedback summaries
- Participation in sprint reviews and planning
- Release communication and documentation review
- Incident updates and customer communication

### Interactions with Other Roles
- **Product Managers**: Share customer insights and prioritization input
- **QA**: Report bugs and validate fixes from customer perspective
- **Developers**: Provide real-world usage context and edge cases
- **DevOps Engineer**: Coordinate during incidents and releases
- **UX Designer**: Provide user feedback on usability issues
- **Project Managers**: Report on customer impact and readiness for releases
